smallweb.site

this is a small site powered by a series of google spreadsheets made by talyssa

these sheetsites are built with a few intentions:
gather, visualize, share

in the veteran stages of the internet, it seems more important to figure out ways to engage more intimately. with the entire world completely accessible at all times, i find it difficult to feel any sense of real identity online. building personal websites has become a safe haven for combatting this digital depersonalization.
aside from this collection, i also maintain a digital garden which has a handful of resources and notes on small/niche online spaces
to learn more about sheet sites, i'd encourage you to refer to this tutorial by chia amisola
