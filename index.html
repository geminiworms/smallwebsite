ethos

this site is built with a few intentions:

gather, play, share

in the veteran stages of the internet, it seems more important to figure out ways to engage more intimately. with the entire world completely accessible at all times, i find it difficult to feel any sense of real identity online. building personal websites has become a safe haven for combatting this digital depersonalization.
