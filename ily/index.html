this is an ongoing project about love

it started as an exercise i'd do in my notes app to ground myself off of blanket statement, band-aid "i love yous"...
but it had the unexpected benefit of helping me practice self love (sorry, buzzword, dont know what else to call it though) with more refined intentions and greater specificity. it came up again months later when my dear friend kristina told me she was "struggling with
cheap i love yous in the highs of new love"... i shared this with her and it snowballed into a spreadsheet of all the alternate ways you can say it.
